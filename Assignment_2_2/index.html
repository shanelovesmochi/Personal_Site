Geographical Distribution of Bike Accidents in NYC

This interactive map visualizes bike accident locations in New York City using a heatmap. The data is based on reported accidents involving bicycles, providing insights into high-risk areas.

I use google colab and python to initially process the data: filter, sum, csv to geojson, etc. The geojson files are visualized in visual studio code. Here are the sources.

Motor Vehicle Collisions - Person

https://data.cityofnewyork.us/Public-Safety/Motor-Vehicle-Collisions-Person/f55k-p6yu/about_data

2025-01 City Bike Data

https://citibikenyc.com/system-data
